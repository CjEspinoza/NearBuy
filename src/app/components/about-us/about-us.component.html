ABOUT NEAR BUY

Meet the Founders!

Hevenn Fuerte and Rondel Hallare came up with the idea when the demand for delivery services was at its peak. Wanting to create a delivery service that is convinient and cheaper than other delivery services, while meeting high excpectations, the Near Buy delivery service was born.

Near Buy aims to deliver the store at your doors, making it safe and convinient for customers. With a wide array of products to choose from, almost if not all needs of the consumer is met.
