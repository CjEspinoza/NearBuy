Step by step guide

  • Start filling up your cart in the Store
  • Fill up the Payment Form
  • Track your order
  • Enjoy your Grocery!

With a wide range of products to choose from, you can select your desired item, Once selected, it will be stored in your personal cart. You can also check your selected products. Once done, you can now proceed to checkout your items and fill up the payment form. Near Buy also lets you track your order, ensuring product safety and reliability. Near Buy delivery service ranges from PNPA to Muzon. When the order is delivered, all that is left is for you to enjoy your grocery!
